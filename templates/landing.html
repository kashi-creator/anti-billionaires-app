05Questions

Before you apply.

Is this just another mastermind? +
No. Masterminds end. The Society is a permanent network. Calls, deal flow, brotherhood, and a private platform built specifically for operators. You are not buying a course. You are joining a society.
What does it cost? +
$99 / month, ongoing. There is no fixed end to the membership. You can cancel any time. The Society also offers Lifetime Access, but you don't buy it, you earn it. Bring three brothers into the Society. Once each one has completed six paid months ($594 each), your billing ends permanently and your seat is yours for life.
Who is this for? +
Men who are already operators, builders, or serious about becoming one. Founders, investors, professionals, tradesmen, fathers. Anyone allergic to the soft, sanitized version of modern manhood.
Who is this NOT for? +
Tourists. Spectators. Men looking to be entertained instead of forged. If you're not ready to show up, contribute, and be held accountable, stay where you are.
How do I get in? +
Apply through the membership page. Sign up with your card and your $99/month begins. We reserve the right to remove members who don't show up, but we'd much rather have you stay.
Can I cancel? +
Yes, any time. $99/month continues until you cancel or until you've referred three brothers who each completed six paid months. At that point your billing ends and your access becomes lifetime.
